Results of the photographic project and their use in a possible Ad campaign for Lush Cosmetics

The beauty of imperfections

The aim of the course was to conceive a photographic project with a possible practical application. The idea was to create a campaign for the brand Lush Cosmetics based on the value of authenticity, relevant to both their products and, therefore, their communication.
Through this projects I wanted to bring out those "imperfections" that are usually removed so I decided to enhance them by framing them with flowers, usually associated to beauty and femininity.
